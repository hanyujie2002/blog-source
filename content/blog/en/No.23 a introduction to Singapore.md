# An introduction to Singapore

Singapore is a famous country located in South East Asia which is a developed country with the **fastest Internet speed**, the **lowest corruption** and the **prosperous economy** among the four tigers of Asia.

The temperature of Singapore is constant throughout one year. Singapore has a tropical rainforest climate. Since then, it's a wonderful destination for traveling in winter.

Singapore is ranked a financial center all over the world. Famous for it's low tax politics, Singapore is seen as a tax heaven, with many companies there. 

Singapore is called the "garden city" for it's park widespread over there and "Little Red Dot" for that Singapore is always depicted as a small red point in the world map. Singaporean are proud of that Little Red Dot title for that this title shows that the influence of Singapore is not matched with its land area.

Lots of tourist attractions are in Singapore including Jewel Changi Airport where there is a beautiful inner waterfall there.

Taxi are popular in Singapore than private car due to that the lowest Taxi fee in developed countries and the hard restriction on private car.

The fertility rate in Singapore is 0.8, the lowest among developed countries.
