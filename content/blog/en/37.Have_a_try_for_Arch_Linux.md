# Have a try for Arch Linux

Among large amount of Linux distributions, which one is best? This is a controversial problem. However, more and more programmers are selecting Arch Linux as their favorite and here is why.

*There are three main reasons.*

## Reasons

### 1. Arch Wiki

Arch Wiki is quite famous among Open source world, including even those who don't use Arch Linux as that contents of Arch Wiki are very rich, and the computer field(mainly practice accompanied by theories) are covered comprehensively by it.

### 2. AUR(Arch User Repository)

The AUR is very strong as that the software in it are uploaded by users, and it contains almost all the software you need.

### 3. Rolling update

Rolling update is the means by which Arch Wiki update. This is a bit controversial as that this results to the fact that Arch Linux is not stable enough to handle commercial service and not suitable for Server. However, by rolling update, one could enjoy the newest software every day, which is liked by me.

## Concluding

Arch Linux is a great Linux and you should try it on your personal computer but not your VPS.
