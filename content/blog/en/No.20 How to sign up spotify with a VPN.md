# How to sign up Spotify with a VPN

![20220529205153](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hanyujie2002/my_pictures/main/images/20220529205153.png)

## Background 

Since some reasons this or that, you have to sign up Spotify with a VPN. However, while registering, you are warned that you have been detected using a VPN and you are banned from signing up which is so frustrated.

## Ans

Just click the "sign up" button continuously and after the button was clicked more than ten times, succession will come.
